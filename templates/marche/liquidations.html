How to read this page

🔴 Long liquidated

Buyers were forced out. The market hit their stop loss. Often creates a sharp wick down — watch for institutional absorption and reversal.

🟢 Shorts sold out

Short sellers were squeezed. This creates forced buying that accelerates upward moves — but often reverses once the squeeze is exhausted.

⚡ High impact events

Events above $5M on a single asset in a short window. These move markets. When clustered, they signal a structural level being tested.

🌊 Cascade pattern

Multiple high-impact events on the same asset in under 10 minutes. Often precedes a reversal as the flush clears the path for the next move.
