FAQ

Common questions

Does each level truly have different content — or is it the same repeated?
Each level introduces entirely new concepts not covered in previous levels. Level 1 covers foundations. Level 2 introduces BOS, order blocks and liquidity sweeps — none of which appear in Level 1. Level 3 adds multi-timeframe planning and inter-market correlations. Level 4 teaches institutional desk logic. Level 5 covers statistical edge validation. Level 6 adds Python quantitative analysis. Level 7 covers professional capital management. There is no content repetition between levels by design.
Do I need to complete Level 1 before accessing Level 2?
You can access any unlocked level at any time. However, the curriculum is designed as a genuine progression — concepts in Level 3 assume knowledge from Level 2, and Level 4 builds directly on Level 3. We strongly recommend completing each level in order for maximum benefit.
What is the Master Certification?
The Master Certification is awarded after completing Level 7's final exam — a comprehensive assessment covering all 7 levels. It is the capstone credential of the VelWolef Academy, demonstrating mastery of the complete curriculum from trading foundations to professional capital management.
Do I need to know Python for Level 6?
No prior coding experience is required. Level 6 includes a complete Python setup module designed for traders with zero coding background. You will learn to run, read and modify existing scripts rather than write code from scratch. All scripts are provided with full explanations.
Is Level 7 relevant if I am not planning to manage external capital?
Even for personal traders, Level 7 provides highly relevant content: prop firm evaluation strategy, funded account risk management, track record construction, and the professional review frameworks. Many traders use Level 7 to prepare for prop firm challenges — the funded account risk management module alone justifies the level for serious traders.
Can I upgrade from Basic to VIP directly?
Yes — you can upgrade to any plan at any time. If you upgrade from Basic to VIP, all 7 levels are unlocked immediately including Levels 4, 5, 6 and 7.
