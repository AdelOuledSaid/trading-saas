Common questions

Can I cancel at any time?
Yes — absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. No engagement, no penalty. Your access remains active until the end of the billing period you paid for.
How quickly do I get access after paying?
Immediately. As soon as payment is confirmed via Stripe, your account is upgraded and all features of your plan are unlocked — usually within 30 seconds.
What exactly are the trading signals?
Each signal includes: the asset, direction (BUY/SELL), precise entry price, stop loss level, take profit target, timeframe, and a structural analysis of why the setup is valid. Premium signals also include execution context and mini-courses linked to each trade.
Is the Academy a replacement for courses I paid for elsewhere?
The VelWolef Academy is a 7-level, 84-module structured curriculum — more comprehensive than most standalone trading courses sold for €200–€500. It's included in your subscription, not a separate purchase. And unlike most courses, it is directly integrated with the signals and market tools you use daily.
What is whale tracking and why does it matter?
Whale tracking monitors large on-chain and derivatives transactions — movements by institutional or large-wallet participants that retail traders typically cannot see. When whales accumulate or distribute, price often follows with a lag. Knowing this before the price reacts is one of the clearest edges available to retail traders.
Can I upgrade from Basic to Premium or VIP later?
Yes — you can upgrade at any time. The price difference is prorated, and your new features are unlocked immediately. Many members start with Basic to test the platform and upgrade within the first month.
Is this suitable for complete beginners?
Yes. Level 1 of the Academy is free and requires no prior trading knowledge. The Basic plan gives you real structured signals while you learn. The curriculum is designed so that each level builds on the previous one — you are never thrown into advanced concepts without the foundation.
